# Passive Ant — Audio Prompts (Batch)

This document contains detailed audio prompts and filenames for a future AI audio generation tool. These prompts are designed to maintain the game's characteristic **cozy/brutal contrast**—blending organic, warm acoustic colony management with sudden, stylized, and crunchy combat sounds.

---

## Background Music & Ambience Loops (4 planned)

### 1. Calm Colony Background Loop
* **Filename**: `audio_colony_calm_loop.ogg`
* **Type**: Loop (30 seconds, seamless)
* **Aesthetic**: Cozy, warm, organic, slow-paced.
* **Prompt**: 
  > A cozy, warm, and gentle 30-second seamless background loop for an ant colony management screen. Organic and acoustic instrumentation featuring soft marimba taps, light kalimba plucks, and a slow, warm acoustic guitar pattern. Underlaid with subtle ambient outdoor sounds: distant wind rustling through grass, soft soil scratching, and organic forest clicks. No heavy percussion, no modern synthesizers, no epic strings. Calm, peaceful, and looping perfectly.

### 2. Campaign Map & Siege Ambience Loop
* **Filename**: `audio_campaign_ambience.ogg`
* **Type**: Loop (30 seconds, seamless)
* **Aesthetic**: Adventurous, slightly tense, outdoor.
* **Prompt**: 
  > A gentle yet adventurous 30-second seamless ambient loop for an outdoor tactical trail map in an insect strategy game. Features a soft acoustic guitar melody, hollow log percussion tapping a slow steady rhythm, and atmospheric outdoor sounds: soft rustling leaves, distant crickets, and a gentle breeze. Slightly mysterious but warm, hinting at the open garden frontier. Loops seamlessly.

### 3. Starvation Hunger Warning Loop
* **Filename**: `audio_hunger_warning.ogg`
* **Type**: Loop (15 seconds, seamless)
* **Aesthetic**: Anxious, sparse, warning, low-intensity.
* **Prompt**: 
  > An anxious and sparse 15-second seamless loop to indicate food shortage and hunger warning in a cozy strategy game. Low, pulsing bass drone, thin and slightly dissonant acoustic guitar notes plucked slowly, and dry leaf-rustle textures. Keeps a tense, warning character without being loud, modern, or overly synthetic.
